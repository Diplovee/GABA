Zimbabwe's tech scene is getting a major boost with the opening of a new innovation hub in Harare. The facility will provide workspace, mentorship, and funding opportunities for local entrepreneurs.

The hub, located in the central business district, features modern co-working spaces, meeting rooms, and a dedicated makerspace with 3D printers and other prototyping equipment.

## Supporting Local Talent

"We're seeing incredible talent in Zimbabwe," said the hub's director. "Our goal is to provide the infrastructure and support these entrepreneurs need to turn their ideas into successful businesses."

The facility has already attracted interest from several international investors looking to support African tech startups.

## What's Next

The hub will officially open its doors next month with a startup showcase event featuring presentations from 10 local tech companies.
