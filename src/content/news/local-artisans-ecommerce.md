A growing number of Zimbabwean artisans are discovering the benefits of e-commerce, selling handcrafted goods to customers across the globe.

From intricately woven baskets to stone sculptures, traditional Zimbabwean crafts are finding appreciative audiences in Europe, North America, and Asia.

## Digital Transformation

"Before, we relied on tourists and local markets," says Grace Mutendi, who runs a beadwork cooperative outside Bulawayo. "Now we can reach customers everywhere through online platforms."

## Challenges Overcome

物流 and payment processing were initial hurdles, but partnerships with local logistics companies and international payment platforms have made cross-border sales more accessible.

The trend is creating new income opportunities for rural communities and helping preserve traditional craftsmanship for future generations.
