FarmFresh bridges the gap between Zimbabwe's rural farmers and urban families seeking fresh, locally-grown produce.

## Features

- Browse products from local farms
- Order fresh produce for delivery
- Track your order in real-time
- Support local agriculture

## Tech Stack

Developed using React Native for cross-platform compatibility (iOS and Android).

## Mission

Reduce food waste, support local farmers, and provide urban residents with access to fresh, affordable produce.
